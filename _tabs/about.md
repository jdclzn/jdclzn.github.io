# Drafts & Dragons

Welcome to **Drafts & Dragons**, my personal developer blog.

Here, I share:

- Insights and **practical lessons on software development**
- Deep dives into **modern web technologies, frameworks, and architectural patterns**
- Experiences building **SaaS platforms, Laravel applications, and Vue.js frontends**
- Guides on **Docker, CI/CD, cloud deployment, and scalable systems**
- Reflections on **life, learning, and personal growth**

---

## What I Write About

### 🔹 Software & Web Technologies
I explore **best practices, language features, and tools** that help write **clean, maintainable, and efficient code**.  
Topics include **PHP (Laravel), JavaScript (Vue.js), TypeScript, APIs, and full-stack development**.

### 🔹 Architecture & Patterns
Discussions on **Clean Architecture, Domain-Driven Design (DDD), microservices, and software scalability**.

### 🔹 SaaS & Enterprise Projects
Lessons learned while building **multi-tenant SaaS platforms**, integrating **backend and frontend workflows**, and managing production deployments.

### 🔹 DevOps & Docker
Practical guides for **containerization, CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure automation, and cloud-ready deployments**.

---

## Personal Life & Thoughts

Coding is only part of life — here, I also share **personal reflections and experiences**:

- Lessons I’ve learned while balancing **career, learning, and hobbies**
- Thoughts on **teamwork, problem-solving, and creativity**
- Moments of inspiration, curiosity, and challenges in life beyond the keyboard

I believe that **personal growth fuels professional growth**, so I try to share insights that resonate both as a developer and as a human.

---

## Who This Blog Is For

- Developers seeking **practical examples, reusable patterns, and real-world tips**
- Tech enthusiasts exploring **modern web stacks, frameworks, and software architecture**
- Learners curious about **enterprise-grade applications and SaaS platforms**
- Anyone interested in **personal reflections, life lessons, and growth alongside coding**

---

## How I Write

I treat each post like a **mini journey**:

1. Share context and goals
2. Break down code examples and architecture decisions
3. Highlight best practices and lessons learned
4. Include personal reflections when relevant

Whether it’s **technical tutorials, project write-ups, or personal thoughts**, everything is written with clarity, honesty, and real-world practicality in mind.
